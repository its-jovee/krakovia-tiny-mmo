## Usage Instructions

### Adding Nodes to Your World

1. Open your instance map scene (e.g., `overworld.tscn`)
2. Click "Add Child Node" (Ctrl+A)
3. Choose "Instantiate Child Scene"
4. Navigate to: `source/server/world/components/harvesting/nodes/`
5. Select the desired node (e.g., `miner_node_t1.tscn`)
6. Position it in your world
7. Adjust radius if needed in the inspector

### Recommended Placement Strategy

**Starter Areas (T1 nodes):**
- Place near spawn points
- Easy accessibility
- All three class types available

**Mid-Level Areas (T2-T3 nodes):**
- Require some exploration
- May be guarded by enemies
- Progressive difficulty

**Advanced Areas (T4-T5 nodes):**
- Dangerous zones
- Remote locations
- High-level enemy presence

**Legendary Areas (T6 nodes):**
- End-game zones only
- Extremely rare
- Maximum challenge

### Visual Identification

Each node has a unique color modulation for easy identification:
- **Miner nodes**: Brown/Orange tones (darkening with tier)
- **Forager nodes**: Green tones (darkening with tier)
- **Trapper nodes**: Tan/Brown tones (consistent across tiers)
